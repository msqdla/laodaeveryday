Decameron – Day I, Story 5

Editor’s merge: English (J. M. Rigg, 1903, public domain) + Chinese (上海译文出版社 译本，方平/王科一 等).
English (J. M. Rigg, 1903)
The Marchioness of Montferrat, by a banquet of hens seasoned with witty words, checks the mad passion of the King of France.
Source: J. M. Rigg, The Decameron of Giovanni Boccaccio, 1903.
中文引文（上译社：方平 / 王科一 等）
“夫人，难道这里全是母鸡，公鸡一只也没有吗？”
